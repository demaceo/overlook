About Us

Hotel California provides guests with a beautiful place to stay, travel, and work abroad indefinitely. We use our in-depth local knowledge to offer a range of unique and authentic experiences. From the center of the city to the breathtaking national parks, we put our heart, soul, and wanderlust into providing families, travelers, and friends with a new and exciting way to explore the land we know and love.

Amenities


Choose from one of our many fully furbished and newly remodeled rooms. Our selection includes single rooms, suites, junior suites, and residential suites.

Each boudoir includes:
• Your selection of a double, queen or king size bed with satin sheets that are sewn right before your check-in
• Premium high-speed internet sent down your personal SpaceX satellite
• An 80" HD TV with millions of stations from around the globe
• A Gordon Ramsay-approved marble kitchen with cookware

Services

Given COVID-19, we adjusted our services so that we may still provide the best possible experience to our guests.

The gym, pool, sauna, yoga studio, meditation garden, ballroom, bar, and public kitchen are still available for a limited number of guests at a time. Speak with the concierge at the front desk for further details and slot reservations.

We understand that this may be an inconvenience to our guests so, as a way to further compensate for these unprecedented times, we now offer remote cooking classes and Guided Meditations completely free of charge!

Each activity is scheduled every Monday, Wednesday, and Thursday at noon and again at 5:30pm. Guests can attend by either reserving a spot at our public kitchen or by simply viewing our live channel (#402 for Cooking, #503 for Meditation) at the schdeuled time.
